Vitra Color Matching

Skyline Design's innovative and eco-friendly back-painted glass, incorporates vibrant color with the classic beauty of architectural glass. With low-VOC and water-based paint production processes, Vitracolor sustainably ensures a uniform coating for each glass panel and offers virtually unlimited color possibilities.
